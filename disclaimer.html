Disclaimer

This spreadsheet is intended to be used as a guide, not an exact savings calculator. There's not an exact science!

  • All pricing comparisons are estimates based on regular menus available at the time
  • Prices are calculated using averages, not the most expensive items (although sometimes a sample of the most expensive item was used)
  • Portion size is factored in when clearly stated on the RW menu (ex: RW 6oz filet vs 8oz, adjusted proportionally)
  • Vegetarian / vegan menus consistently show lower value due to regular menu pricing
  • If a menu has 4+ courses, courses may be combined across appetizers / entrees for consistency
  • Some menus have brand-new or RW only items and can't be directly compared
  • "Too Different To Tell" = menu didn't have a clean comparison or brand new menu
  • Restaurants that do not list dessert are of higher value in general - often more substantial food
  • Some restaurants were manually adjusted based on menu quality, structure, or missing pricing... sometimes personal bias too
  • Removed all of Nando's + Lou Malnati's
